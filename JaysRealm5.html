College, finally seemed like a place where I could be "me".

But it took a turn for something worse.

I wasn't expecting manipulation and pettiness by professors.
I got falsely accused again, this time I fought back. But I still had to leave the group I helped to form.
It was my safe place. I didn't want to, but I had no choice. Rumors flew around that I tried to sabotage it.
Rumors flew around that I loved a teacher. Well, it was she who lashed out first..
Was freshman me threatening, in my oversized "Pokemon" hat and awkward smile?
