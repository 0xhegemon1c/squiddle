My Hacking Journey as a Neophyte

I have always been interested in computers, and interested in hacking. With three of my family members being programmers and one family member being in IT, my interest was innate.
However, I began my journey with only one goal in mind:

Learn everything that I possibly can.

The deeper that I got into my obsession,
the more invested I became and the more I refused to give up when finding out that the rabbit hole was much deeper than I had originally thought.
I learned that "hacking" was far from easy, and I became enamored with (and sucked into the life of) technology and hacking.
I started out as a script kiddie before I realized that nothing in life that is worth doing is ever easy.
My journey led me through OSINT, HAM radio, building electronics, writing malware, and attending local Def-Con meet-ups.
