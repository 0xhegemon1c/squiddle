After her death, the mental health issues set in.

Anxiety, depression, OCD, mania, chronic insomnia.

My days were driven by anguish over these invisible issues.
Whisked away to India to be hopped up on drugs instead of placed in therapy.
So I didn't learn how to be okay until after I turned 20 and got off the drugs and improved my life myself.
Until then, I faced isolation half a world away, years of self-harm, and an addiction.
I had nobody to hear me. I spent hours alone in the dark.
